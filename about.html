Who we are

RooMate is an online friendship, companionship and matchmaking platform built in Assam, for India. We help adults (18+) discover genuine connections — friendship, serious relationships, live-together companionship ("RooMate"), and marriage-minded matching — all on one safe, respectful platform.

What makes us different

  • Women make the first move. In man–woman connections, only the woman can send the first message. This single rule removes the harassment problem that keeps many Indian women away from dating apps.
  • Safety built in. Phone numbers and personal contact details are automatically blocked in chat. Every member can report or block another member instantly, and verified profiles carry a ✔ badge.
  • Made for Tier-2 and Tier-3 India. Affordable subscription pricing, regional focus, and a dignified, family-values-friendly approach — not a hookup app.
  • Group Plans. Members can meet safely at public group meetups and local events rather than one-to-one first meetings.

Our services and pricing

Creating a profile, browsing and matching on RooMate is free. Women receive 2 free messages in every connection.
