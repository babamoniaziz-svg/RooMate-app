Privacy Policy

Last updated: 03 July 2026 · Applies to the RooMate web application

1. Who we are

RooMate ("we", "us") is a relationship and companionship platform operated by Md. Aziz Ali Choudhury, Baihata Chariali, Kamrup (Rural), Assam, India. Contact: Contact & Grievance page.

2. Information we collect

(a) Account data: name, email address, password (stored in encrypted/hashed form by our authentication provider), age, gender, city, and relationship intent you select. (b) Profile data: bio, photos and other details you choose to add. (c) Activity data: likes, matches, messages sent within the platform, blocks and reports. (d) Technical data: basic device/browser information needed to operate the service.

3. How we use your information

To create and operate your account; to show your profile to other members as part of the core matching service; to deliver chat between matched members; to enforce our safety rules (including automated filtering of phone numbers and contact details in chat); to process Premium subscriptions; to respond to reports and grievances; and to comply with Indian law.

4. What we never do

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your phone number or email with other members. We do not display your profile outside the platform.

5. Storage and security

Data is stored with our backend provider (Supabase) on cloud infrastructure. Access is protected by row-level security so members can only access data they are entitled to see. Passwords are never stored in plain text. No system is 100% secure; please use a strong, unique password.

6. Your rights (DPDP Act, 2023)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 you may request: access to your personal data, correction of inaccurate data, erasure of your data, and grievance redressal. To exercise these rights, use the Contact & Grievance page. Account deletion requests are honoured within 30 days; some records may be retained where required by law (e.g., safety reports).

7. Children

RooMate is strictly for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. Accounts found to belong to minors are deleted.

8. Cookies and local storage

We use browser local storage to keep you logged in and to remember app settings. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

9. Third-party services

Authentication, database and file storage: Supabase. Payments (when enabled): Razorpay — your card/UPI details are processed by Razorpay and never touch our servers. Hosting: Vercel.

10. Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change and material changes will be notified in-app.

11. Grievance Officer (IT Rules 2021)

Grievance Officer: Md. Aziz Ali Choudhury — see Contact & Grievance. Complaints are acknowledged within 24 hours and resolved within 15 days as per the Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021.
