Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 03 July 2026

1. Subscription cancellation

You may cancel RooMate Premium at any time from the app or by contacting us. Cancellation stops future renewals; your Premium benefits continue until the end of the already-paid period.

2. Refunds

(a) Failed or duplicate payments: if money was deducted but Premium was not activated, or you were charged twice for the same period, the excess amount is refunded in full within 5–7 working days of verification. (b) Change of mind: subscription fees for a period already started are non-refundable, since digital benefits (unlimited messaging, SuperSwipes, Compliments, Premium modes) are delivered immediately on activation. (c) Account banned for violating Terms: no refund is due for the current period.

3. How to request a refund

Contact us via the Contact & Grievance page with your registered email, date of payment and payment reference (Razorpay payment ID from your bank/UPI app). We acknowledge within 24 hours and resolve within 7 working days.

4. Mode of refund

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method through Razorpay. Bank posting times may add 2–5 working days.

5. Pricing

All prices are in Indian Rupees (INR) and shown inclusive of applicable taxes at the point of purchase, currently ₹99/month for RooMate Premium. Prices may change prospectively; existing paid periods are never re-charged.
